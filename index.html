Like Andal who dreamed of the divine union — Kanaa Kanden thozhee naan — we too have dreamed of this blessed day. With hearts overflowing with gratitude and joy, we humbly invite you to witness and bless the sacred union of Rahul Babu and Suruthi.

As Andal sang of a thousand elephants heralding the arrival of Lord Narayana, may your presence be the greatest blessing that graces this celebration. Join us as two families, two hearts, and two souls come together in an eternal bond — witnessed by the heavens and celebrated with all the love in the world.
