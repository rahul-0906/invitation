With Gracious Hearts
✦

Like Andal who dreamed of the divine union — Kanaa Kanden thozhee naan — we too have dreamed of this blessed day. With hearts overflowing with gratitude and joy, we humbly invite you to witness and bless the sacred union of Rahul Babu and Suruthi.

As Andal sang of a thousand elephants heralding the arrival of Lord Narayana, may your presence be the greatest blessing that graces this celebration. Join us as two families, two hearts, and two souls come together in an eternal bond — witnessed by the heavens and celebrated with all the love in the world.

Your blessings, your laughter, and your presence are the finest gifts we could ever receive on this most auspicious of days.

Nachiyar Thirumozhi

Andal’s Dream — Varanam Ayiram

நாரண நம்பி நடக்கின்றான் என்று எதிர்

Vaaranam aayiram soozha valam seithu
Naarana nambi nadakkinraan enru ethir
Poorana porkudam vaithup puram engum
Thoranam naattak kanaa kanden thozhi naan

“I dreamt, O friend, that a thousand elephants
formed a grand and majestic procession,
and Lord Narayana walked in their midst —
golden pots filled to the brim were placed before Him,
and festive garlands adorned every direction.”

— Āṇṭāḿ · Nācciyār Tiromoḷi · Vāraṇam Āyiram
